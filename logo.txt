# SonicKraft Logo Design Prompts

Use these prompts in Gemini, ChatGPT, or Midjourney to generate a professional logo for your project.

---

### OPTION 1: Minimalist & Modern (The "S.K." Icon)
**Prompt:** 
"A minimalist, high-end logo for a music technology brand named 'SonicKraft'. The logo should feature a sleek, stylized 'SK' monogram using bold, geometric sans-serif typography. Incorporate a subtle musical element, like a small soundwave or a digital bit-stream, integrated into the letters. Colors: Deep slate grey background with a vibrant emerald green (#10B981) and electric indigo gradient. Style: Clean, flat design, vector art, professional, tech-startup aesthetic."

### OPTION 2: The "Sonic Pulse" (Abstract & Dynamic)
**Prompt:**

### OPTION 3: Futuristic Glassmorphism (Premium App Icon)
**Prompt:**
"A premium 3D app icon logo for 'SonicKraft'. A translucent glass square with rounded corners (glassmorphism style). Inside the glass, a glowing, metallic emerald green 'S' and 'K' intertwined. Subtle floating music notes around the icon. Soft, cinematic lighting, frosted glass texture, deep blue and black background. The design should look identical to a high-end macOS or iOS app icon."
